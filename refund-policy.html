Jess Carter Refund Policy

Refund Policy

We want every customer to feel confident about their purchase. Please review our refund policy and request procedures below.

Refund Eligibility

You have up to 7 days after payment confirmation to request a refund.

Refund requests must be submitted within 7 calendar days of the original purchase date.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please send an email to:

support@jesscarter.online

Use the following subject line:

Refund – Order #XXXX

Please include the following information:

  • Your full name
  • Your purchase email address
  • Your order number

Refund Method & Timeline

Once approved, refunds will be processed using the same payment method used during the original purchase.

Refund processing may take up to 7 business days after approval, depending on the payment provider and financial institution involved.

Exceptions

  • Refund requests submitted after the 7-day refund period will not be accepted.
  • Purchases made in error are subject to the same refund request procedure and deadline.
  • Incomplete refund requests may require additional information before review and approval.
