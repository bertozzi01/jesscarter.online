Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data responsibly. By accessing or using our services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

Legal Basis

Personal data is processed in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including the Brazilian General Data Protection Law (LGPD).

Legal Basis:
• Performance of a Contract (Art. 7, V, LGPD)
• Compliance with Legal Obligations

• Consent, when required

Personal information is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.
